Introduction

Dynob is the AuScope Dynamic observing tool developed by Lim Chin Chuan at the University of Tasmania, Australia to automate the AuScope VLBI observing processes.

Program structure

Dynob can be used to automate each part (scheduling, correlation, data processing, and performance monitoring) separately, or the entire AuScope VLBI process loop shown in the figure below.
